## Presskit checklist

### Must-have (publishable in 1 evening)
- One-liner + short pitch (25–40 words)
- Factsheet (genre, platform, status/version, dev, location, tech)
- 6–10 screenshots (at least 1 clean UI shot + 1 big battle)
- 3 raw clips (5–12s each)
- Links:
  - Play: `/play/`
  - Story: `/het-verhaal/`
  - Donate: `/doneer`
  - Roadmap: `/roadmap`
  - GitHub: repo link
  - Contact email

### Nice-to-have (week 2–4)
- Trailer (30–60s)
- B-roll bundle (1080p, raw gameplay)
- Logo pack (png + svg)
- Key art
