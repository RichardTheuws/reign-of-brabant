## Privacy notice draft (donations / pure tips)

> Status: draft. Keep it simple, minimal data, no marketing without explicit opt-in.

### Who we are
Reign of Brabant is developed by Richard Theuws.

### What this page is for
The donation flow is a **voluntary contribution** (tip). We use a payment provider to process payments securely.

### What data is processed
- **Payment processing data**: handled by the payment provider (e.g. Stripe or Mollie). Depending on the payment method this can include transaction identifiers, payment status, and (for some methods) payer name/bank details.\n- **Site analytics**: (only if enabled) we should keep this privacy-friendly and avoid collecting unnecessary personal data.\n- **Newsletter**: only if you explicitly opt in. No pre-ticked boxes.

### Why we process data (purposes)
- **To process your payment** and show success/failure outcome.\n- **Bookkeeping**: store only what is required for accounting and legal obligations.\n- **If you opt in**: send you updates about Reign of Brabant (newsletter).

### Legal basis (high-level)
- Payment processing and basic administration: necessary for the donation transaction / legitimate interests (depending on final setup).\n- Newsletter: consent (opt-in).

### Data sharing
Payments are processed by a payment provider. The provider acts as a processor for payment data.

### Retention
We retain bookkeeping-relevant records for as long as required by applicable law. We do not intentionally store raw payment details beyond what is necessary.

### Your rights
You can request access, correction, deletion (where applicable), and you can withdraw newsletter consent at any time.

### Contact
For privacy requests: `richard@theuws.com`
