## Risks & mitigations per rung (draft)

### Global scope rules
- Donations are “best effort” toward milestones; no guaranteed delivery dates.
- Each rung is timeboxed; unfinished items roll forward.

### €1k risks
- **Risk**: polish work is invisible.
  - **Mitigation**: publish “before/after” clips and a short changelog post.

### €5k risks
- **Risk**: AI/pathing turns into endless tuning.
  - **Mitigation**: define acceptance criteria and stop once met.

### €10k risks
- **Risk**: performance targets depend on hardware variance.
  - **Mitigation**: define test device class + reproducible benchmark scene.

### €25k risks
- **Risk**: tooling work delays “player-visible” content.
  - **Mitigation**: ship tooling in slices; each slice unlocks 1 visible improvement.

### €50k risks
- **Risk**: campaign implies a large narrative/content commitment.
  - **Mitigation**: explicitly “1 slice only” with clear non-goals.
